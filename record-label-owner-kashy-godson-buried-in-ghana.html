While crucial investigation is going on to piece together what led to the death of Record label owner, Kashy Godson, the family of the deceased has laid his remains to rest in Ghana.

According to the reports, the late founder of Cash Nation Records, died on Sunday, March 7, 2021.

While some allege he jumped from his house balcony after battling depression for a long time, others alleged he was pushed off the building by someone he had a business transaction with.

Kashy would have turned 27 today, March 12.
